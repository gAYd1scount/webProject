Library clerks are similiar to librarian, but they mainly focus on the conditions of the library's facility and equitment. Other than that, they play a part in repairing damaged books. If the equipment is beyond repair, they will report it to their supervisor to order a replacement.

* Salary=5250 MYR per month

Skill Required:

  • Collecting fines
  • Shelving books
