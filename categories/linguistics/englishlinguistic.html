Author: Leslie Dunton-Downer | Published: 2010

Synopsis

In "The English is Coming!", Leslie Dunton-Downer delves into the fascinating journey of the English language, exploring how it has become a global phenomenon. From its origins to its current status as a worldwide lingua franca, the book discusses the spread of English, its influence on other languages, and the challenges it faces in the modern world.
