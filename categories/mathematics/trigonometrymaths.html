Synopsis

"Trigonometry: A Complete Guide" by Charles P.Mckeage explores the principles and applications of trigonometry, from the basic trigonometric ratios to more advanced topics such as inverse functions and the law of sines and cosines. This book is suitable for both beginners and advanced learners.
