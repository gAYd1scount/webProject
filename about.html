ABOUT US

Our History

Founded in 2000, Libranet has been a cornerstone of the Malaysian community for over 24 years. What started as a small collection of books donated by local residents has grown into a comprehensive library system with thousands of volumes, digital resources, and community programs.

Our Mission

At Libranet, our mission is to foster a love for reading and learning by providing free access to a diverse collection of resources, supporting lifelong learning, and enriching the lives of our community members. We believe in the transformative power of knowledge and aim to empower individuals through our services and programs.

Our Vision

We believe that a strong community is built on the foundation of shared experiences and mutual support. Our vision is to be a welcoming and inclusive space where people of all ages, backgrounds, and interests can come together, engage in meaningful dialogue, and forge lasting connections.
