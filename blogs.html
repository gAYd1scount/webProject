The Importance of Mental Health

The Mental Health of participants in Academia is a crucial topic to attend to, from Students to Lecturers, their contribution towards the learning environment should be attended with care. A healthy and sustainable journey is as important as reaching for the skies. Affordable therapy sessions are of importance in order to tackle this issue, our organization is happy to aid by providing financial aid to individuals in need. For all.

Student-Teacher Engagement

Student-to-Teacher engagement is crucial for a clear and steady path in students' learning journey. Encouraging their participation in class and within the realm of education contributes to their passion and love for the syllabus and skills taught in courses. Teachers from all corners of the world are appreciated and remembered by students as to their lasting contribution to their growth and spirit in life. The Centre for Universal Education plans on adopting a new standard for students to grow with their teachers.

Lowering the Barrier of Entry

Education is key to a growing society, it plays an integral role for children and young talent to nurture their talent and ability in varying branches from sciences to arts. The cost of education should remain affordable and realistic for up and coming students to allow them to expand their horizon in life. That is why the Centre for Universal Education gives out 100s of scholarships on a yearly basis to students all around the world.
